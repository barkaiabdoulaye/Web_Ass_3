# Stakeholders - SMEStack

## Primary Stakeholders

### 1. SME Owners
- **Needs:** Affordable websites, reliable developers, clear progress tracking
- **Pain Points:** Limited budget, no technical knowledge, bad past experiences

### 2. Students
- **Needs:** Real projects for portfolio, practical experience, income
- **Pain Points:** No access to clients, need guidance

### 3. Admin (Platform Manager)
- **Needs:** Monitor platform, ensure quality, match students to projects
- **Pain Points:** Manual oversight, ensuring student readiness

## Secondary Stakeholders
- INES Ruhengeri (institution)
- Local community
- Future employers
