# Problem Definition - SMEStack

## Current Situation

**For SMEs:**
- Need websites and digital services
- Cannot afford professional agencies (cost: 1,000,000 - 5,000,000 RWF)
- No way to find student developers
- Risk of scams or incomplete work

**For Students:**
- Graduate with theory but no practical experience
- Need portfolio projects for jobs
- No access to real clients during studies

## Why This Matters
- SMEs lose customers without online presence
- Students struggle to find jobs after graduation
- Local economy needs digital transformation
